Rules

  • Red plays first.
  • Each player in his turn selects a column in which the lowest available space will be filled.
  • The turns alternate until one of the players gets four checkers of his colour in a row. The four in a row can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  • The first player to get four in a row wins.
  • If the board is filled with pieces and neither player has 4 in a row, then the game is a tie.
